Who are we?

Oliver Baby Store Limited is a specialized store selling all kinds of produts related to babies until 5 years old.

The store has opened on November 2022 and is located in Av. Julius Nyerere n. 547 ground floor, in Maputo, Mozambique.

The main trademarks are Chicco and Johnson.

The products can be ordered online and be pickup by the store or be delivered.
